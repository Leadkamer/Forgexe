# Blog Afbeelding Prompts — Nano Banana

Stijl voor alle afbeeldingen: **Minimalistisch, dark mode, tech/terminal esthetiek. Forgexe kleuren: donkere achtergrond (#0a0b0e), groen accent (#34d399), amber accent (#f5a623). Clean, modern, B2B-professioneel. Geen stockfoto-look. Denk aan Vercel/Linear visuele stijl.**

Formaat: 1200x630px (OG image formaat, werkt ook als blog thumbnail)

---

## 1. Hoe AI-agents je salesteam versterken zonder te vervangen
**Bestandsnaam:** `blog-img-ai-agents-sales.png`

**Prompt:**
Minimalist dark illustration, nearly black background (#0a0b0e). Center: a human figure silhouette in white/light gray sitting at a desk. Next to the human: a glowing green (#34d399) digital figure/hologram representing an AI assistant, slightly transparent. They are working together — the AI is handing documents/leads to the human. Subtle green glow lines connecting them. Terminal-style monospace text watermark "FORGE.GROWTH" very faintly in the bottom corner. Clean, professional, no text overlays. Moody tech atmosphere with subtle grid lines in background.

## 2. 5 processen die elk MKB-bedrijf vandaag kan automatiseren
**Bestandsnaam:** `blog-img-5-processen.png`

**Prompt:**
Minimalist dark illustration on near-black background. Five abstract geometric shapes arranged in a horizontal row, each representing a business process: a clock (time), a document/invoice, a chat bubble, a bar chart, and a handshake/onboarding icon. Each shape glows with a different color: green (#34d399), amber (#f5a623), cyan (#06b6d4), blue (#3b82f6), violet (#8b5cf6). Connected by subtle flowing dashed lines between them. Clean flat design, no gradients, monoline style. Dark tech aesthetic.

Minimalist dark illustration. Center: a CRM icon (abstract database/contact book shape) in amber (#f5a623). ## 6. Waarom je AI-strategie begint bij je bestaande tools
**Bestandsnaam:** `blog-img-ai-strategie-tools.png`

**Prompt:**
Minimalist dark illustration. Three horizontal layers stacked vertically, like a building foundation. Bottom layer (largest, green #34d399): labeled with tool icons (CRM, email, calendar) — your existing tools. Middle layer (medium, amber #f5a623): automation/workflow arrows connecting the tools. Top layer (smallest, cyan #00f0ff): a brain/AI icon glowing at the peak. Suggests building AI on top of what you already have. Dark background, clean geometric blocks, subtle glow effects. Professional, strategic feel.

---

## Algemene tips voor Nano Banana:
- Voeg toe aan elke prompt: "dark background, minimalist, clean, professional B2B, no text, 1200x630 aspect ratio"
- Als de output te druk is: voeg "negative space, simple composition, fewer elements" toe
- Als het te generic is: voeg "tech terminal aesthetic, monospace grid overlay, subtle glow" toe
- Test in zowel light als dark mode van de site — de afbeelding moet op beide werken
